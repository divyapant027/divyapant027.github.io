Recognition & Awards

From academic honors to international stages

August 6, 2022 · Melrose Ballroom, New York City

Miss Nepal North America 2022

2nd Runner Up & Miss Talent

At the Miss Nepal North America 2022 pageant held at Melrose Ballroom in New York City, Divya Pant was crowned 2nd Runner Up and received the Miss Talent award, a recognition of her platform advocacy combining sustainability science with cultural representation.

Rather than treating the pageant as an end in itself, Divya used the platform to amplify her work in climate science and women's empowerment in agriculture. Her talent presentation and advocacy messaging centered on the intersection of environmental sustainability and gender equity, themes drawn directly from her doctoral research and community work in Nepal.

The Miss Nepal North America title provided a megaphone for Divya's mission, connecting her with diaspora communities and opening doors for speaking engagements on sustainability and Nepali cultural preservation across the United States.
