Penn State News

November 2022

Graduate student from Nepal creates waste management venture

Divya Pant, a doctoral candidate in biorenewable systems at Penn State, founded Carbon Away to convert organic waste into energy and fertilizer using anaerobic digestion. The venture installed its first digester in Nepal in July 2022, processing up to 30 tons of waste per day.

Growing up in Jhalari, a small farming village in Nepal, Divya Pant witnessed how women sustained agricultural systems without recognition. Now a Penn State doctoral candidate, she's channeling that experience into Carbon Away, a venture turning waste into sustainable energy.
